    Hello! I'm Lindsay and I'm a front end developer. You've probably noticed that I went to Notre Dame (but here it is again since ND grads love talking about it!) and studied computer science and design. Last year I interned at award winning digital agency R/GA in NYC and recently I've been working on a web application that helps irish dancers track their results at competitions. Check out my portfolio and github to see some of my work.

    Speaking of irish dance, I'd be leaving out a large part of my life if I didn't mention my own personal involvement. It has been my passion for the past 14 years. It truly takes blood, sweat, and tears and I wouldn't be the person I am today without it.
