An interactive symbol design game
I see some shapes. What is this? Your goal is to create 6 symbols that fit together as a set. Each symbol should be unique but fit in with the others. Tap in each dotted section to get started.
It says game. So how do I win? There's no one correct solution. The point is to gain a better understanding of how shapes can work toegther to create positive and negative space.
